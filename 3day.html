3 Days Sharing Tanzania Safari

Safari & Tours Overview

3 days safari to Arusha National Park,Tarangile National Park ,and Ngorongoro Crater offers a wonderful opportunity to experience the beauty and wildlife of Tanzania.

Here's an overview of what you can expect from the park:

  • Arusha National Park: Located near the town of Arusha ,this park is known for its diverse landscapes and rich wildlife.On your safri ,you can explore the lush forests,picturesque lakes ,and the imposing Mount Meru.Keep an eye out of animalslike giraffes,buffalo,zebras and various species of monkeys.Birds loverswill also be delighted by the numerous avian found in the park.

  • Tarangile National Park: Situated in northern Tanzania ,Tarangire National Park is famous for its large elephant herds and baobao trees.During your visit ,you'll have the chance to witness elephants,wildebeest,zebras,lions and other

  • Ngorongoro Crater:one o Africa's most iconic safari destinations,the volcaniccaldera that provides a natural sanctuary for a wide array of animals.Inside the crater,you can spot the Big Five ( elephant,lions, leopards,rhinoceros and buffalos) as well as hoppos,zebras,gazelles,and sheer concentraction of wildlife make this a truly unforgettable experience.

Detailed Itinerary

After breakfast,you'll depart from your Arusha or Moshi accommodations and drive to Arusha National Park.

After your entrance to the park,you will embark on a game drive through the park,passing landmarks such as momella Lakes and Ngurdoto Crater-sometimes known as 'Little Ngorongoro'.

Also,your Safari vehicle will pass under the Fig tree(Ficus thonningii) whose roots have grown to take the shape of an arch,large enough for a asafari car(elephant size) to pass underneath.

During lunch ,you will stop at apicnic site and then proceed with the game drive.At around four you will start leaving the national park for Arusha where you will spend the night.

You will have an early start to ensure that we complete the intinery for the day which includes a magnificent game drive with a picnic lunch in the Ngorongoro Crater.Animals infrequently migrate in and out of the crater due to its elevented walls,which means you could spot every member of the big five in one day.

The Big five comprise buffalo ,elephant ,rhinos,lions and leopard.Some of the other wildlife that you can expect to see include zebras, hippos,wildebeest, and hynas. The craters also feature an extensive bird population including geese,strrks and vultures.

You will be graced by the lovely flamingos on soda Lake . Our guides are highly skilled and will impress you will impress you with their ability to track down animals.This is a skill that a book cannot teach you and that a selected few can perform.Your trip will come to an end in the late afternoon and you will proceed to Arusha city / Moshi town
