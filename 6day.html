6 Days Tanzania Classic Safari

Safari & Tours Overview

Welcome to the mesmerizing world of Tanzania ,where adventure a waits at every turn. Are you ready to embark on a thrilling journey through the heart of the African wilderness?

Look no further than the 6 Days Tanzania Classic Safari, a carefully curated experience that will leave you awe-inspired and cravings for more.This article serves as your ultimate guide to this remarkable Safari,offering valuable insights and detailed information that will help you plan the trip of a lifetime.

A Safari Like No Other

Immerse yourself in the untamed beauty of Tanzania's wildlife and narural wonder with the 6 Day Tanzania Classic Safari. This safari itinerary is designed to showcase the best of what Tanzania has to offer,from its vast plains teeming with wildlife to its breathtaking landescapes that will take your breath away.

Detailed Itinerary

Meet our Driver Guide at Kilimanjaro International Airport who is excited to show you the beauty of Tanzania. Afterwards, you will drive straight to the heart of Arusha, to your hotel set among the woodland slopes of Mount Meru.

Overnight stay at Arusha

Meals: Lunch and Dinner

After breakfast, drive along the beautiful view of volcanic landscapes and across the rift valley to Tarangire National Park, the sixth largest of its kind in Tanzania.

Once in the park, you will have an en-route game drive with a picnic lunch; pass by a thick elephant population, baobab trees, lions and also the wildebeest, elands, zebras, buffaloes, and many more migrating animals.

Overnight stay at Lake Manyara

Meals: Breakfast, Packed Lunch and Dinner

Begin your journey to the iconic Serengeti National Park. Pass by villages and adventurous routes and find yourself in the middle of the magnificent Serengeti National Park, known for the wildebeest migration.

Enjoy a game drive till dusk and get busy spotting predators, elephants, giraffes, zebras, and beyond. Enjoy your evening at leisure.

Overnight stay at Serengeti

Meals: Breakfast, Packed/ Buffet Lunch, and Dinner

Begin your day with an optional magical balloon ride or choose to go for game drives at your own pace. The Serengeti National Park is vast and surely you will be tempted for more game drives.

Noted for the wildebeests, you will also get to spot here, huge pride of lions, herds of elephants, hippos, leopards, cheetahs,s and a variety of large and small birds.

Overnight stay at Serengeti

Meals: Breakfast, Packed/ Buffet Lunch, and Dinner

Today will be your most awaited day as our driver guide will take you into the Ngorongoro Conservation Area, featuring a deep, volcanic crater, the largest unflooded and unbroken caldera in the world.

About 20 km across, 600 meters deep, and 300 sq km in area, the Ngorongoro Crater is a breathtaking natural wonder. It is here you are most likely to see the famous Big 5 animals.

Overnight stay at Ngorongoro

Meals: Breakfast, Packed Lunch and Dinner

Rise to the sounds of nature and bid farewell to Ngorongoro. After a rewarding holiday, it's time to head back with all the happiest memories.

Depart to Arusha for your return to home or on-going journey to Zanzibar.
