Arusha National Park

Unique Attractions

Lake Manyara National Park, located in northern Tanzania,
Is famous for its stunning scenery and diverse wildlife. The park features a unique mix of lush groundwater forests, open savannah, hot springs, and the beautiful Lake Manyara itself. It's renowned for tree-climbing lions, large herds of elephants, and flocks of flamingos. Easily accessible from Arusha, Manyara offers excellent game viewing, birdwatching, and breathtaking views of the Great Rift Valley escarpment
